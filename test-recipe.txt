Test-Recipe

Ingredients:
1. Dough
2. Pizza Sauce
3. Mozzela Cheese
4. Pepporini
5. Sausage
6. Canadien Bacon

Instructions:
1. Roll out dough in to circle
2. Add sauce on dough
3. Add cheese
4. Add pepporoni
5. Add Sausage
6. Add Canadian Bacon

1. Preheat over to 425 degrees
2. Cook pizza for 12-16 minutes (or until crust is brown)
3. Cut in to slices
4. Enjoy!
